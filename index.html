All the audio sample use HiFi-GAN as a vocoder. We verified whether the keywords detected by KeyBERT could identify linguistically and acoustically important words in a manner similar to humans. The red color represents the keywords detected by KeyBERT, and the blue boxes indicate acoustically important words with large pitch and duration variations. Here, acoustically important words refer to those with pitch and duration features greater than 0 in the recorded speech, indicating that these words exhibit larger-than-average variations. During the inference process, we apply emphasis by automatically detecting two keywords within a sentence using KeyBERT.
